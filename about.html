Welcome to my art space :)

Hi there! When I’m not busy being a responsible adult, you’ll probably find me elbow-deep in paint, sketching figures, or making a digital mess on my tablet. I dabble in oil painting, watercolor, and digital art—basically, if it involves colors and chaos, I’m in.

You can check out a few of my favorite pieces here, and if you're hungry for more, head over to my Instagram. There you'll find not just art, but also my side quests in knitting, crocheting, and sewing—aka grandma hobbies with millennial flair.

Bonus facts: I play guitar and bass, geek out over architecture, and occasionally try not to lose a finger while woodworking. Thanks for stopping by—enjoy the scroll!
